About Me

Anmol Varma is a Senior Product Manager at Microsoft with over four years of experience in building and launching B2B and B2C products. He specializes in personal and team Copilots, AI agents, product strategy, and improving user engagement. Anmol has a strong technical background with expertise in generative AI, agentic computing, and user interface design.

Before joining Microsoft, Anmol worked as a Senior Software Engineer at Samsung Electronics, where he developed core camera technologies and earned patents in generative AI. He holds an MBA from the Indian School of Business and a B.Tech from the National Institute of Technology Karnataka Surathkal.
